FixMyLinux - FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is FixMyLinux?

FixMyLinux is a small web platform designed to help users diagnose and solve common Linux problems. Rather than being a script or command-line tool, it's a lightweight website: users can browse or search for known Linux issues and find guidance on how to fix them.

How do I use it?

Paste your Linux problem in the text box and click Fix it!

Is it free?

Yes, completely free.
