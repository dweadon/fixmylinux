Frequently Asked Questions

What is FixMyLinux?

FixMyLinux is a web platform designed to help users diagnose and solve Linux problems. Paste an error message, log output, or issue description and receive troubleshooting guidance.

How do I use it?

Paste your Linux problem into the text box on the homepage and click Fix it!

Is it free?

Yes. FixMyLinux is completely free to use.
